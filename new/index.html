About Professor Abdikamalov

Professor Bakhtiyar Abdikamalov is a distinguished physicist and educator at Karakalpak State University in Nukus, Uzbekistan. His contributions span multiple fields, including physics education, scientific translation, and the preservation of Karakalpak cultural heritage. Through his extensive work translating fundamental physics texts into the Karakalpak language, he has made scientific knowledge accessible to Karakalpak-speaking students and researchers. His dedication to both science and culture exemplifies the bridge between modern academic pursuits and traditional heritage preservation.
